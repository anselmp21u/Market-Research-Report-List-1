<p><strong>The Portable Water Penetration Testers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Pressure Difference Method</li><li>Static Pressure Head Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable water penetration testers are essential tools used to assess the water resistance of building materials. The Air Pressure Difference Method involves creating a pressure differential across a sample to gauge water penetration, effectively simulating real-world conditions. In contrast, the Static Pressure Head Method utilizes a column of water to measure the pressure exerted on a material, assessing its ability to resist water ingress. Both methods provide valuable data for evaluating waterproofing performance and ensuring structural integrity in construction.</p></p>
