<p><p>The Off-Road Vehicle Lamps market comprises three primary types: Halogen, HID (High-Intensity Discharge), and LED (Light Emitting Diode). Halogen lamps offer a warm light and are cost-effective but have shorter lifespans. HID lamps produce brighter light with lower energy consumption, making them ideal for rugged terrains. LED lamps are increasingly popular due to their longevity, energy efficiency, and compact size, providing robust illumination and durability for off-road adventures. Each type caters to different preferences for performance and efficiency.</p></p>
