<p><p>The molded goods market primarily includes Liquid Injection Molding (LIM) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). LIM is a manufacturing process that uses liquid silicone rubber to create intricate parts by injecting the material into molds under pressure, ensuring precision and high-quality finishes. LSR refers specifically to the type of silicone used in LIM applications, known for its resilience, flexibility, and heat resistance. Both techniques are widely utilized in sectors such as automotive, medical, and consumer goods, offering reliable and durable components.</p></p>
