<p><ul><li>VRLA Batteries</li><li>Flooded Batteries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-wheeler lead-acid battery market primarily consists of two types: VRLA (Valve Regulated Lead Acid) batteries and flooded batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed and maintenance-free, providing reliable performance and enhanced safety by preventing acid leaks. They are favored for their compact design and suitability for modern two-wheelers. Flooded batteries, in contrast, require periodic maintenance and have a vented design, offering robust performance at a lower cost. Both types are essential for powering various two-wheeler applications efficiently.</p></p>
