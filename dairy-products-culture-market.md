<p><p>The dairy products culture market consists of three main types: Mesophilic, Thermophilic, and Probiotics. Mesophilic cultures thrive at moderate temperatures, essential for cheese and yogurt production, enhancing flavor and texture. Thermophilic cultures operate at higher temperatures, crucial for yogurt and certain cheese varieties, offering unique characteristics. Probiotics are live microorganisms that provide health benefits, often added to fermented dairy products to promote gut health. Each type plays a vital role in the production and nutritional enhancement of dairy foods.</p></p>
