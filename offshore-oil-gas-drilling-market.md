<p><p>The offshore oil and gas drilling market consists of various types of rigs, each suited to different environments and water depths. Jackups are mobile units that operate in shallow waters, featuring legs that elevate the platform above the sea. Semi-submersibles are stable structures buoyed by submerged columns, ideal for deeper waters due to their resistance to harsh conditions. Drillships are advanced vessels equipped for deep-water drilling, offering mobility and sophisticated technology for exploration and production in challenging locations.</p></p>
