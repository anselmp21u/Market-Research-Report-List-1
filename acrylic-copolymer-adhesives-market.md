<p><p>Acrylic copolymer adhesives are classified into three main types based on their physical form: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives are versatile and easy to apply, ideal for bonding intricate surfaces. Solid adhesives offer high strength and durability, often used in applications requiring robust bonding. Paste adhesives provide a thicker consistency, suitable for vertical surfaces or when more control during application is needed. Each type serves distinct applications across industries, ensuring effective adhesion for various materials.</p></p>
