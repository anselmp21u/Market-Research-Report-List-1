<p><ul><li>Cone Mill</li><li>Hammer Mill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical milling equipment market includes various types, primarily focusing on cone mills and hammer mills. Cone mills utilize the principles of sizing by reducing the particle size through a conical screen, ensuring uniformity and minimal dust generation. They are ideal for delicate materials. Hammer mills, on the other hand, employ high-speed rotating hammers to grind and crush substances, providing versatility and efficiency for various applications. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the quality of pharmaceutical products by ensuring proper particle size and consistency.</p></p>
