<p><strong>The Clean Label Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clean Label Pectin market is categorized into High Methoxyl Pectin (HMP) and Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMP). HMP is typically used in products requiring sugar, enabling gelling at higher sugar concentrations, making it ideal for jams and jellies. In contrast, LMP gels with minimal sugar and in the presence of acids, making it suitable for reduced-sugar foods and dairy applications. Both types cater to consumer demand for natural ingredients and transparency in food labeling, enhancing product appeal.</p></p>
