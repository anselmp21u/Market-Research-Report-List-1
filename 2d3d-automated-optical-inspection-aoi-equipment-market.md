<p><p>The 2D Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment employs high-resolution cameras to capture images of electronic components and PCBs, focusing on surface defects and misalignments in two dimensions. In contrast, 3D AOI equipment uses advanced imaging technology, such as laser triangulation and structured light, to create a volumetric representation of the components, enabling more comprehensive inspections that can detect defects in height and shape. Both types are crucial in enhancing quality control and improving manufacturing efficiency in electronics.</p></p>
