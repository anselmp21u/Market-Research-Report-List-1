<p><p>The female urinary incontinence products market is categorized into three main types: Stress Incontinence, Full Urinary Incontinence, and True Incontinence. Stress Incontinence involves leakage during physical activities that put pressure on the bladder, prompting the need for supportive pads or briefs. Full Urinary Incontinence entails a complete loss of bladder control, often requiring specialized products such as adult diapers. True Incontinence is characterized by constant urine leakage, necessitating highly absorbent products to manage continuous flow and maintain comfort.</p></p>
